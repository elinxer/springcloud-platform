
## 引入说明

使用下面方式引入

```
<dependency>
    <groupId>com.elinxer</groupId>
    <artifactId>springcloud-platform-test</artifactId>
    <scope>test</scope>
    <exclusions>
        <exclusion>
            <groupId>org.junit.vintage</groupId>
            <artifactId>junit-vintage-engine</artifactId>
        </exclusion>
    </exclusions>
</dependency>
```
